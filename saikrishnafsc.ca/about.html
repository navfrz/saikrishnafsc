The People Behind the Flavour

Our Founders

Raman Sobti

Co-Founder & Head Chef

With over two decades of culinary expertise rooted in North India, Raman brought authentic Punjabi samosa recipes to Canada. His passion for traditional cooking techniques and bold flavours is the heart and soul of every samosa we craft.

Shikha Sobti

Co-Founder & Operations

Shikha manages operations and business growth with unwavering dedication, turning a humble home kitchen venture into a thriving wholesale empire. Her vision for excellence and community has been instrumental in Sai Krishna Foods' success across Atlantic Canada.

Our Journey

Our Story

What started as a simple dream to share authentic North Indian flavours has grown into Atlantic Canada's most trusted samosa brand. Founded by Raman and Shikha Sobti, Sai Krishna Foods began in a modest home kitchen, fuelled by passion and a family recipe perfected over decades. Today, we proudly manufacture thousands of handmade samosas every week, serving grocery chains, restaurants, and communities across the region.

2017

Moved to Canada

The Sobti family arrived in New Brunswick with a vision to bring authentic Indian cuisine to Atlantic Canada.

2019

First 200 Samosas

Debuted at a local farmers market with 200 handmade samosas that sold out within hours, sparking overwhelming demand.

2020

Expanded to Wholesale

Growing demand led to the transition from farmers markets to wholesale distribution, partnering with local retailers.

2021

Supplying All Sobeys NB

Became a trusted supplier to all Sobeys locations across New Brunswick, a major milestone in our growth.

Now

Atlantic Canada's Leading Samosa Manufacturer

Operating from a state-of-the-art facility, we are the premier handmade samosa manufacturer serving all of Atlantic Canada.
