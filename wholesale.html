Wholesale Supply

Your Trusted Wholesale Samosa Partner

Sai Krishna Foods is Atlantic Canada's premier wholesale manufacturer of authentic handmade samosas. We supply grocery chains, restaurants, convenience stores, and food service providers with consistently high-quality products made from traditional North Indian recipes.

Our state-of-the-art production facility ensures we can meet your demand at any scale, from small boutique orders to large chain-wide distribution. Every samosa is handcrafted with natural ingredients and zero preservatives, giving your customers an authentic taste experience they'll come back for.
