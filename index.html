OUR STORY

A Journey of Passion & Flavor

In 2017, Raman and Shikha Sobti moved from Dubai to Canada with a dream and a cherished family recipe. What started as making samosas in their garage quickly turned into something extraordinary. Their first batch of 200 samosas sold out at a local farmers market, and word spread fast. Today, Sai Krishna Foods proudly supplies Sobeys and businesses across Atlantic Canada, crafting every samosa by hand with the same love and tradition that started it all.
